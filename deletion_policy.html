Madurai Malli Account Deletion Policy

1. Account Deletion Request:

  • Users can initiate the account deletion process by sending an email to the designated account deletion email address: help.kavisit@gmail.com.

2. Email Format:

  • In the email subject, users should mention "Account Deletion Request for Madurai Malli."
  • In the body of the email, users should include the following information:
    • Username/Email associated with the account.
    • Any additional account information that may be required.

3. Confirmation:

  • Users will receive an email confirming the receipt of their account deletion request.

4. Two-Step Verification (if applicable):

  • If the user has enabled two-step verification, they may need to provide additional information to verify their identity.

5. Verification Process:

  • The support team may reach out to the user via the registered email or other means to verify the account deletion request.

6. Grace Period:

  • A grace period may be implemented to allow users to reconsider their decision. During this period, the account remains active, and users can cancel the deletion request by following the instructions in the confirmation email.

7. Irreversible Deletion:

  • After the grace period, the account deletion becomes irreversible. Users will be notified once the account is permanently deleted.

8. Retention of Certain Information:

  • The app may retain certain information for legal, security, or business reasons even after an account is deleted. Users should be informed about the specific types of data that may be retained.

9. Changes to the Policy:

  • The mobile app reserves the right to update or modify the account deletion policy. Users will be notified of any changes, and it is their responsibility to review the updated policy.

10. Contact Information:

  • If users have questions or concerns about the account deletion process, they can contact our support team at help.kavisit@gmail.com.
